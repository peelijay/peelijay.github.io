ABOUT

Javeria Hasnain is a Brooklyn-based Pakistani poet, translator, and educator. Her poems and prose have appeared widely, most recently, in Pleiades, Poet Lore, The Brazenhead Review, and Foglifter. She is currently a Fulbright scholar and an MFA Poetry candidate at The New School, and was selected as a 2023-24 Educational Associate at Teachers & Writers Collaborative. Her interests lie at the intersection of education and literary arts. She has previously worked with Cave Canem Foundation, The Adroit Journal, and Habib University in both administrative and editorial capacities and currently serves as a Manuscript Reader for Tupelo Press.
